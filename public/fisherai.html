Frequently Asked Questions

Is FisherAI completely free to use?

Yes, FisherAI is completely free to use with the included free models. You can also connect your own API keys for premium models if desired.

Is my data safe with FisherAI?

Absolutely. FisherAI stores your API keys locally on your device, and the code is completely open source for transparency and security.

Which video platforms are supported?

FisherAI currently supports YouTube and Bilibili for video summarization and subtitle translation/downloading.

Can I use FisherAI in my language?

Yes, FisherAI supports multiple languages including Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, German, French, and Russian.
